## The Five Metrics CT Actually Respects (Ranked)

### 1. Engagement Rate (Weighted by Account Size) — A+
- Most honest signal of actual influence
- Foresight uses: `sqrt(avgLikes + avgRetweets×2 + avgReplies×3) × 1.5`
- CT loves: Weights replies 3x (discourse > passive consumption)
- Verdict: Perfect

### 2. Follower Growth Rate — A
- New followers = active choice (harder to fake)
- Foresight uses: `1 pt per 2K followers + 5 pts per 1% growth`
- CT loves: Rewards both absolute scale and momentum
- Verdict: Very good (could detect spike anomalies)

### 3. Activity Consistency (Not Just Volume) — A
- Daily presence shows reliability
- Foresight uses: `min(35, tweets_this_week × 1.5)` cap prevents spam
- CT loves: Thoughtfulness over ADHD tweeting
- Verdict: Good, cap prevents abuse

### 4. Spotlight Bonuses (Community Voting) — B+
- Community judges callout accuracy
- Foresight uses: `[12, 8, 4] pts` for top 3 voted
- CT loves: Recognizes what metrics can't capture
- Verdict: Good, needs clear voting mechanics

### 5. Viral Tweet Detection — B
- Identifies real impact moments
- Foresight uses: Tiered (10K=4pts, 50K=7pts, 100K=12pts, max 3)
- CT loves: Doesn't overweight one viral week
- Verdict: Works, but estimation rough without tweet-level data
