# Foresight Logo Generation Prompts

**Purpose:** AI-optimized prompts for generating 5 distinct logo concepts for Foresight
**Target:** Ideogram, Midjourney, DALL-E
**Brand Context:** Fantasy sports game for Crypto Twitter — dark theme, gold accent, premium/sharp aesthetic
**Constraints:** Must work at 32px (favicon size), no purple, crypto-native audience

---

## Design Brief Summary

**Product:** Foresight - Draft fantasy teams of CT influencers, predict engagement, compete for ETH

**Visual Identity:**
- Primary Color: Gold/Amber (#F59E0B)
- Background: Dark (#0A0A0F)
- Style: Minimal, geometric, professional
- Vibe: "Premium crypto trading meets fantasy sports" — sharp, data-driven, ownable
- Metaphor: "Ability to see the future" (prediction, foresight, clarity)

**Technical Constraints:**
- Legible at 32px (favicon)
- Works on dark backgrounds (primary use case)
- Symmetrical or nearly symmetrical (for rotation in brand applications)
- No gradients that degrade at small sizes
- Solid lines / flat design preferred
- Must avoid generic crypto/sports imagery

## Concept 1: The Clarifying Eye

**Concept:** A stylized eye aperture that opens/resolves, symbolizing clarity and prediction. The iris contains a forward-looking chevron or upward trajectory. Works as both a mark and a wordmark anchor.

**Why This Works:**
- "Foresight" = "ability to see" — eye is literal + metaphorical
- Geometrically strong at small sizes (concentric circles become recognizable)
- Gold accent can glow from center outward
- Unique vs. generic eye logos (which are everywhere)
- Pairs well with a wordmark

**Midjourney/Ideogram Prompt:**

```
A sleek, minimal logo mark of an EYE APERTURE, geometric and abstract.
The eye is formed by 3 concentric circles with a thin gold horizontal line cutting through the iris.
Inside the pupil is a small sharp UPWARD CHEVRON or ASCENDING ARROW in gold, suggesting clarity and prediction.
The outer circle is dark charcoal, the middle circle is slightly lighter, creating depth.
All lines are crisp and sharp, no blur or gradients.
Style: vector logo, tech company aesthetic
Color: Gold (#F59E0B) accent, charcoal/dark gray backgrounds, black outline
Format: Square logo mark, isolated on dark background
Must be legible at 32px
Inspiration: Aperture science, modern crypto trading apps, DeFi clarity
NO cartoonish, NO photorealistic, NO gradients, NO purple
Minimal, sharp, ownable
```

**Design Notes:**
- Circle-based = scalable to any size
- Horizontal dividing line = "seeing across (time/data)"
- Chevron = forward motion
- Consider: Can this become an animated spinning aperture? (app icon potential)

## Concept 2: The Probability Lattice

**Concept:** An abstract grid or lattice pattern that forms the letter "F" in negative space. The grid suggests data, prediction, and computational thinking. Gold nodes punctate the corners. Crypto-native without being obvious.

**Why This Works:**
- "F" for Foresight — lettermark is most ownable
- Grid = data, prediction, neural networks (subtle but recognizable to crypto audience)
- Lattice pattern holds together at small sizes (multiple points of visual weight)
- Stands alone OR works with wordmark
- Scalable and simple

**Midjourney/Ideogram Prompt:**

```
A minimal, geometric LETTERMARK logo of the letter "F" constructed from a thin geometric lattice or grid pattern.
The "F" is formed by the negative space between dark lines, not filled.
At the 6 intersection points of the grid (particularly the corners), small perfect circles in gold (#F59E0B) accent the nodes.
The overall style is tech-forward, suggesting data, prediction, and computation.
Lines are thin and elegant, dark gray on dark background.
Style: vector logo, minimal, tech/crypto aesthetic
Color: Dark charcoal lines (#27272A), gold node accents (#F59E0B), on black background
Format: Square logo mark, must be legible at 32px and larger
Inspiration: DeFi protocol logos (Aave, Compound), neural networks, prediction markets
NO solid filled shapes, NO gradients, NO text labels
Clean, ownable, distinctive
```

**Design Notes:**
- Negative space = sophisticated (not filled/heavy)
- Gold nodes = data points, value, precision
- Works well as favicon (geometric = clear at small sizes)
- Wordmark option: "Foresight" in clean sans-serif below/beside

## Concept 3: The Ascending Trajectory

**Concept:** A single ascending line or arc that morphs into an upward-pointing chevron. Suggests prediction, trajectory, and growth. Minimal and kinetic. Could incorporate a subtle circular element (planet/sphere) on the trajectory.

**Why This Works:**
- Represents "foresight" as prediction of upward movement
- Works for fantasy sports (scoring/winning) + crypto (predictions of price)
- Single-line design = ultra minimal, scalable, iconic
- Kinetic energy = matches brand personality
- Can be animated (SVG potential)

**Midjourney/Ideogram Prompt:**

```
A minimal, dynamic logo MARK consisting of a single elegant curved line that ascends from lower-left to upper-right.
The line begins as a gentle arc and resolves into a sharp upward-pointing CHEVRON or ARROW tip at the top.
Along the ascending path at mid-point, a small perfect circle (like a planet or data point) sits ON the line in gold.
The line itself is gold (#F59E0B), approximately 3-4px stroke width.
The overall composition suggests prediction, growth, and forward momentum.
Style: vector logo, single-line design, kinetic and modern
Color: Gold (#F59E0B) on dark background (#0A0A0F)
Format: Square canvas, centered, must work at 32px height
Inspiration: Climbing charts, prediction curves, crypto trading trajectories, ascending planets
NO filled shapes, NO gradients, NO complex details
Elegant, minimalist, feels like it could animate upward
```

**Design Notes:**
- Stroke weight is critical: too thin = disappears at 32px, too thick = loses elegance
- Chevron end = "forward prediction"
- Circle on line = "data point in trajectory"
- Consider: SVG animation for loading states or app launch

## Concept 4: The Crystalline Prism

**Concept:** A geometric prism or crystal-like shape that suggests light splitting into clarity. Composed of 3-4 angular facets meeting at a point. The shape suggests depth and refraction. Crypto audiences respond to crystal/gem metaphors (scarcity, value, rarity).

**Why This Works:**
- Crystals = valuable, rare (appeals to crypto audience)
- Prism = "seeing light clearly" (foresight metaphor)
- Geometric = scalable and sharp
- Can be filled (bold) or outlined (elegant)
- Distinct from eye/arrow logos

**Midjourney/Ideogram Prompt:**

```
A minimal, geometric logo MARK of a three-dimensional CRYSTAL or PRISM shape.
The prism is composed of 3-4 angular facets meeting at a sharp upper point, like a gemstone or diamond cut viewed from a front-ish angle.
The front face is filled with dark transparent gradient (inner), outlined in gold (#F59E0B).
The side facets are slightly darker, creating depth and dimension without looking 3D-rendered.
The overall shape is symmetrical and centered, with a pointed apex facing upward.
Style: vector logo, geometric, angular, precious
Color: Gold outline (#F59E0B), dark charcoal fill (#27272A), deep black background
Format: Square mark, centered, must read as "shiny crystal" at 32px
Inspiration: Crypto gem logos, precious materials, refraction and clarity, luxury brands
NO photorealism, NO complex shading, NO gradients that degrade
Angular, sharp, valuable-looking
```

**Design Notes:**
- Facets = multiple perspectives, multiple data points
- Upward point = aspiration, clarity
- Gold outline = premium feel
- Consider: Does this need a subtle inner glow at larger sizes?

## Concept 5: The Orb of Clarity

**Concept:** A perfect sphere or orb with concentric rings and an inner constellation pattern. Suggests a crystal ball (fortune/prediction) reimagined as a data-driven, crypto-native object. The constellation inside = "seeing stars" = ultimate clarity/insight.

**Why This Works:**
- Crystal ball = prediction (on brand)
- Sphere = complete, whole, balanced
- Constellation = data points, crypto wallets, network nodes
- Can be subtle or bold depending on execution
- Works well at all sizes due to symmetry

**Midjourney/Ideogram Prompt:**

```
A minimal, symmetrical logo MARK of a perfect SPHERE or ORB with an inner CONSTELLATION pattern.
The sphere is outlined in thin gold (#F59E0B) lines forming concentric rings (3-4 rings total).
Inside the center, a constellation of small gold dots/stars connects with thin lines, forming an abstract geometric pattern like a network or connected nodes.
The constellation should feel like "seeing into the future" or "mapping data."
The background inside the sphere is dark but slightly lighter than the outer background, creating subtle depth.
Style: vector logo, geometric, astronomy-inspired, crypto-native
Color: Gold lines (#F59E0B), dark charcoal interior, black outer background
Format: Perfect square, centered, must be legible and beautiful at 32px (favicon size)
Inspiration: DeFi protocols, crystal balls reimagined for crypto, planetary systems, data networks
NO photorealistic sphere, NO bright gradients, NO cartoonish stars
Clean vector, minimal, sophisticated
```

**Design Notes:**
- Concentric rings = layers of data/insight
- Constellation = interconnected information = blockchain
- Symmetry = iconic (works from any angle)
- Consider: Subtle animation of constellation twinkling?

## Implementation Guidelines

### For All Concepts:

1. **Aspect Ratio:** Square (1:1) — essential for app icons and favicons
2. **Scalability Test:** Export as vector (SVG) and verify legibility at 32px, 64px, 128px, 256px
3. **Dark Background Test:** Apply to both #0A0A0F (dark) and #12121A (slightly lighter) backgrounds
4. **Color Variations:** Create versions in:
   - Gold on dark (#F59E0B on #0A0A0F)
   - White on dark (for subtle contexts)
   - Dark on white (for print)
5. **No Rasterization:** Maintain vector format — no PNGs generated until final version locked
6. **Mobile Test:** Visualize at actual favicon size in browser tab
7. **Brand Consistency:** Pair chosen mark with wordmark in "Foresight" (using Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans)

### Evaluation Criteria:

| Criterion | Weight | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Recognizability at 32px** | 30% | Must be readable as favicon |
| **Ownable & Distinctive** | 25% | Not generic; doesn't copy crypto/sports logos |
| **Brand Alignment** | 20% | Feels premium, sharp, crypto-native |
| **Scalability** | 15% | Works from 32px to 1000px without degradation |
| **Animation Potential** | 10% | Can it move? (SVG, loading states, etc.) |

### Recommendation:

Start with **Concept 1 (Eye)** or **Concept 5 (Orb)** as primary options:
- **Eye:** Most directly metaphorical ("seeing the future"), most distinctive
- **Orb:** Most versatile (works at all sizes, animation potential, crystal ball metaphor strong in CT culture)
