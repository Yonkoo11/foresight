## WHAT WE ARE BUILDING

I'm building **Foresight** — a fantasy sports game for Crypto Twitter (CT). The concept is simple: players draft teams of 5 real CT influencers, earn points based on those influencers' actual Twitter activity during the contest week, and compete against other players for prizes. Think DraftKings or FPL (Fantasy Premier League) but for Crypto Twitter personalities instead of athletes.

**The core loop:**
- Every week a new contest opens (Monday → Sunday)
- Players pick 5 CT influencers from a roster of 100, staying within a 150-point budget
- One of those 5 is named Captain and gets a 2× score multiplier
- Points are calculated from each influencer's real Twitter activity: how much they tweet, how much engagement they get, whether they go viral, and whether they're growing their audience
- Players compete on a leaderboard and the top finishers win prizes

**Why CT is the perfect sport for this:**
CT is already tribal. Bitcoin maxis, ETH builders, Solana degens, macro traders — they all have strong opinions about whose calls are better, who's the real alpha source, whose framework is correct. The game just puts a scoreboard on arguments that are already happening. When someone's "team" of CT analysts outperforms another person's team, they brag about it on CT. Others see it, disagree with the team selection, and join to prove their roster is better. That's the viral loop.

**Who plays this:**
- Active CT users who already follow and have opinions about these influencers
- People who enjoy the "I was right about [person]" satisfaction of being early to a good call
- Anyone who argues in CT replies about whose analysis is better
- Fantasy sports players who are also in crypto

**The business:**
- Free tier: play in free weekly contests
- Paid contests: entry fees with prize pools (we take a rake)
- Premium subscription for advanced analytics, draft tools
- Revenue starts with the paid contests, scales with the user base

**This is a real startup, not a demo.** The product is launching imminently and needs a genuine, credible roster of 100 CT influencers that CT people will immediately recognize and respect — not a curated list of "famous crypto people" but the actual voices that CT communities follow, argue about, and take alpha from daily.

## HOW THE GAME WORKS

### Team Structure
- 5 influencers per team: **1 Captain (2× point multiplier) + 4 regular picks**
- **Budget: 150 draft points** — each influencer has a price based on their tier
- Weekly contests (Mon → Sun), scores update 4× per day
- Players compete on leaderboards for prizes

### Scoring Formula
```
Weekly Score = Activity + Engagement + Growth + Viral

Activity Score (max 35 pts):
  - 1.5 points per original tweet (retweets don't count)
  - Optimal: ~23 original tweets/week
  - Rewards consistent, frequent original content

Engagement Score (max 60 pts):
  - Weighted formula: Replies×3 + Retweets×2 + Likes×1
  - Square root applied to prevent single viral tweets dominating
  - Rewards accounts where people RESPOND and ENGAGE, not just like

Growth Score (max 40 pts):
  - Absolute follower gain + % growth rate combined
  - Fast-growing smaller accounts can outscore large stagnant ones
  - Rewards momentum and narrative building

Viral Score (max 25 pts):
  - Bonus points for tweets breaking 10K engagements
  - Big bonus for truly viral moments (100K+ engagements)
  - The "lottery ticket" mechanic — unpredictable but high upside

Captain Multiplier: Captain's total × 2.0
```

### What Makes a Good Fantasy Pick
**Engagement rate matters far more than follower count.** A 150K account with 8% engagement will outscore a 2M account with 0.3% engagement most weeks. The ideal fantasy pick:
- Tweets 15-30× per week consistently (Activity points)
- Gets replies, quote tweets, and heated debate (Engagement points)
- Growing audience momentum right now (Growth points)
- Occasionally drops a tweet that breaks CT (Viral points)

## THE FIVE ARCHETYPES (tag each influencer with one)

Understanding these helps players make strategic picks:

**Activity Beast** — tweets 25-40× per week, massive Activity score floor, reliable but rarely explosive. Great for consistent baseline scoring. Captain risk: dependable but rarely tops the weekly chart.

**Engagement Wizard** — might only tweet 10× per week but every tweet generates 500+ replies and massive quote tweet discourse. Replies×3 weighting makes them dominant. The "slow and steady" premium pick.

**Growth Machine** — follower count growing fast right now, either because of a recent right call, a viral moment, or building narrative momentum. Great for Growth score. These are the "buy low" opportunities in our game.

**Viral Sniper** — infrequent tweets but occasionally drops something that breaks CT — market call that lands perfectly, a thread that gets ratio'd into mainstream consciousness, or a take that everyone argues about for a week. Unpredictable, high ceiling.

**All-Rounder** — consistent across all four categories. Never dominates a week but never collapses either. The safest Captain pick. Usually the A-tier anchors.

- Content style: Charts, setups, on-chain interpretations, real-time commentary on moves
- Engagement pattern: Massive engagement when they call something right, brutal ratio when wrong

**6. VC / Institutional / Founders**
People building or funding things at the protocol level. They tweet less but when they do, it matters. Their content shapes narratives that ripple through CT for weeks.
- What they value: Product differentiation, real usage, sustainable economics, team quality
- Content style: Ecosystem commentary, portfolio updates, meta-level takes about where crypto is going
- Engagement pattern: Lower tweet frequency but each tweet gets deep engagement from smart followers

## WHAT MAKES THIS GAME GO VIRAL ON CT

This is important for understanding who belongs in our roster:

**Controversy drives gameplay.** When people disagree about whether someone is S-tier or C-tier, they play the game to prove their point. Include people who CT has strong OPINIONS about — both love and hate. "Why is [X] A-tier? They've been wrong about everything for 2 years" is a conversation that makes people join and play.

**Right calls create bragging rights.** If a player drafts someone who then drops a massive alpha call that lands, they win that week AND they have a story to tell on CT. The game creates a reason to share.

**Influencers themselves might join or tweet about it.** At our stage, if even 2-3 influencers tweet "apparently I'm in a fantasy game where people can draft me" — their followers will flood in. The roster should include people who have a sense of humor about CT culture and might engage with this.

**Faction loyalty is a retention mechanic.** Bitcoin maxis will keep playing if they can prove their BTC analysts outperform "the ETH shills." This cross-faction competition is built-in virality.
