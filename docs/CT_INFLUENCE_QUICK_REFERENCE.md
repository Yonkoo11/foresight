## What Makes a Week "Good" vs "Bad"?

### Good Week Drivers (High Engagement)
1. **Market volatility >5%** — 3-5x engagement spike
2. **Protocol governance drama** — Governance nerds engage heavily
3. **Token launches / ecosystem news** — "First to analyze" wins
4. **Controversy / drama** — CT feeds on it
5. **Memes / cultural moments** — 2-3x engagement if timed right
6. **Validation from OGs** — @Vitalik retweets your take = 5-10x spike

### Bad Week Drivers (Low Engagement)
- Market flatlines (no urgency)
- Influencer goes silent (vacation, focus on building)
- Their recent prediction proved wrong
- Major story outside crypto (election, war)
- Only personal-life tweets (no analysis)

### Virality Predictability Assessment
**60% predictable** (market volatility, scheduled launches, governance votes)
**40% chaotic** (memes, drama, random ratio'd tweets)

## Five Metrics CT Actually Respects (Ranked)

### 1. **Engagement Rate (Weighted by Account Size)** — A+
- Why: Honest signal of actual influence
- Foresight: `sqrt(avgLikes + avgRetweets×2 + avgReplies×3) × 1.5`
- CT Love: Weights replies 3x (discourse > passive consumption)
- Verdict: Perfect

### 2. **Follower Growth Rate** — A
- Why: New followers = active choice, harder to fake
- Foresight: `1 pt per 2K followers + 5 pts per 1% growth`
- CT Love: Rewards both absolute and relative growth
- Verdict: Very good, could detect spike anomalies

### 3. **Activity Consistency (Not Just Volume)** — A
- Why: Daily presence shows reliability
- Foresight: `min(35, tweets_this_week × 1.5)` caps spam
- CT Love: 23+ tweets/week = same score (prevents ADHD tweeting)
- Verdict: Good, cap prevents abuse

### 4. **Spotlight Bonuses (Community Voting)** — B+
- Why: Delegates callout accuracy to community
- Foresight: `[12, 8, 4] pts` for top 3 voted
- CT Love: Recognizes that metrics can't capture everything
- Verdict: Good, but needs clear voting mechanics

### 5. **Viral Tweet Detection** — B
- Why: Identifies real impact moments
- Foresight: Tiered (10K-49K = 4pts, 50K-99K = 7pts, 100K+ = 12pts)
- CT Love: Doesn't overweight one viral week
- Verdict: Works, but estimation rough without tweet-level data

## The One Thing to Get Right Before Launch

**CALLOUT ACCURACY**

This is what CT cares about most. The formula measures activity, engagement, and growth perfectly. But it can't measure accuracy.

**Recommendation:** Use community spotlight voting to surface accuracy. Top 3 voted = people who made good calls that week.

Without addressing accuracy, the game is complete. With it, the game is *legitimate*.
