## What is Foresight?

Fantasy sports for Crypto Twitter. You draft a team of 5 CT influencers, they earn you points based on their real Twitter activity that week (how much they tweet, how much engagement they get, whether they go viral, whether they're growing). You compete against other players for prizes.

Think Fantasy Premier League but instead of footballers, you're picking Saylor, Lyn Alden, and Ansem.

The game lives or dies on this roster being credible to real CT people. Not "famous crypto people" — actual voices that CT communities follow, argue about, and take alpha from. That's why I need your eyes on this. You know better than any algorithm who CT actually listens to right now.

---

## How Tiers Work

```
S-Tier (4 people)  — The untouchables. Cross-faction legends.
A-Tier (16 people) — Elite specialists. The best voice in their niche.
B-Tier (30 people) — The active core. CT knows them, tweets constantly.
C-Tier (50 people) — Rising stars and value picks. The interesting ones.
```

Each tier has a draft point price. Players have 150 points to build a 5-person team. Higher tier = more expensive = expected to score more points. Strategy is picking the right mix — you can't afford 5 S-tiers.

Scores are based on: original tweets per week + engagement (replies count 3×, retweets 2×, likes 1×) + follower growth + viral moments. Engagement rate matters more than raw follower count.
