## CURRENT PRICING (PROBLEMS)

### Current Ladder

```
FREE_LEAGUE:       $0      (5 picks, 7 days)
DAILY_FLASH:       $0.001 ETH ≈ $3      (3 picks, 24h)
WEEKLY_STARTER:    $0.002 ETH ≈ $6      (5 picks, 7 days)
WEEKLY_STANDARD:   $0.01 ETH ≈ $30      (5 picks, 7 days)
WEEKLY_PRO:        $0.05 ETH ≈ $150     (5 picks, 7 days)
```

### The Problem: The $10-20 Gap

```
Price progression:  $0 → $3 → $6 → $30 → $150

Gap analysis:
• Free → Daily Flash: 3x jump (people will try)
• Daily Flash → Starter: 2x jump (manageable)
• Starter → Standard: 5x jump ❌ HUGE DROP-OFF HERE

The gap between $6 and $30 is where 40% of potential users live:
- Casual poker players: $10-20/week budget
- Fantasy sports enthusiasts: $15-25/week budget
- Crypto natives willing to risk: $10-50/week budget
```

**Real-world comp:** DraftKings has $1, $5, $10, $20, $50, $100+ entry tiers. Foresight is missing the $10-20 bucket.

## RECOMMENDED PRICING REVISION

### New Pricing Ladder

```
TIER                ENTRY FEE           TEAM   DURATION    PURPOSE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FREE_LEAGUE         $0                  5      7 days      Onboarding
DAILY_FLASH         $0.001 ETH ($3)     3      24h         Engagement
WEEKLY_STARTER      $0.002 ETH ($6)     5      7 days      Conversion
WEEKLY_SWEET_SPOT   $0.005 ETH ($15)    5      7 days      SWEET SPOT ⭐
WEEKLY_PRO          $0.015 ETH ($45)    5      7 days      Power users
WEEKLY_ELITE        $0.05 ETH ($150)    5      7 days      Whales
```

### Why This Ladder Works

**Exponential spacing (each tier ~2.5-3x previous):**
```
$0 → $3 (3x)
$3 → $6 (2x)
$6 → $15 (2.5x) ← FILLS THE GAP
$15 → $45 (3x)
$45 → $150 (3.3x)
```

**Psychological pricing:**
- Users see clear progression
- No unexpected jumps
- Each tier has distinct target user

**Funnel optimization:**
```
100 free players
  ↓ 25% convert to Daily ($3)
  25 daily players
    ↓ 50% convert to Starter ($6)
    12 starter players
      ↓ 40% convert to Sweet Spot ($15) ← NEW
      5 sweet spot players
        ↓ 30% convert to Pro ($45)
        1-2 pro players
          ↓ 10% convert to Elite ($150)
          0-1 elite players
```

**Expected conversion:** 100 → 1-2 at top tier (healthy funnel)

## HOW TO PRICE FOR MAXIMUM MARGIN

### Goal: Increase ARPU Without Losing Volume

**Current ARPU (5K MAU):** $22.80/user/month

**How to increase:**

#### Option 1: Raise Starter Price ($6 → $8)
```
Lost users: 5-10% churn (people use $0 or $15 instead)
Gained margin: +$2/user on remaining 90-95%
Net: -$0.10 to +$0.15 ARPU (minimal gain, not worth it)
```

#### Option 2: Remove Daily Flash, make Starter the minimum paid tier
```
Force minimum entry: $6 instead of $3
Churn: 20-30% (high - many won't convert)
Advantage: Single SKU (simpler UX)
Risk: Lose growth lever for low-confidence players
Verdict: ❌ Not recommended
```

#### Option 3: Add Premium Subscription Option
```
Free tier: Play $0 contests, limited daily plays
Premium ($4.99/month):
  - 2x multiplier on winnings
  - 5x daily contests allowed
  - Early contest access
  - Advanced analytics

Expected adoption: 3-5% of paying players
At 5K MAU, 8% paying = 400 players, 5% adopt premium = 20 players × $4.99 = $100/month

New ARPU: $22.80 + ($100/5000) = $22.82 (marginal improvement)
```

**Verdict:** Premium is nice-to-have later, not core revenue driver now.

#### Option 4: Multi-Entry (Play multiple contests per week)
```
Current: 1 entry per user per week
New: Allow 3-5 entries per week (refill daily)

Volume impact: +2.5x entries if all users max out
Reality: +30-50% incremental entries (not all users will play daily)

Revenue impact: 5K MAU × 1.4 extra entries × $15 sweet spot = +$105K/year

Implementation:
- Add daily refill (e.g., 1 entry/day for 7 days)
- Show calendar UI for easy multi-entry
- Market as "play daily, not just weekly"

Verdict: ✅ HIGH PRIORITY - test in Month 4-5
```

#### Option 5: Dynamic Pricing (Adjust entry fee by contest size)
```
Small contest (< 20 players):   Entry fee + 20% bonus
Medium contest (20-100 players): Entry fee (standard)
Large contest (> 100 players):   Entry fee - 10% discount

Rationale: Encourage joining smaller pools with better odds

Risk: Complexity (users confused by variable pricing)
Verdict: Too complex for MVP, revisit at 10K+ MAU
```

## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY: THE REAL LEVER

### Price Anchoring

When users see the pricing ladder:
```
Free → $3 → $6 → $15 → $45 → $150

They think: "Free is for testing, $15 is the 'normal' price, $150 is insane"
```

**Power:** The $150 elite tier doesn't need to make money - it makes $15 look reasonable!

### The Decoy Effect

Adding a decoy tier can increase conversions:
```
Without decoy:
- Free: 40%
- $6 Starter: 20%
- $30 Standard: 10%
- Conversion to paid: 30%

With decoy ($15 Sweet Spot between Starter and Standard):
- Free: 38% (minimal change)
- $6 Starter: 12% (moved to Sweet Spot)
- $15 Sweet Spot: 18% (decoy effect - people pick middle)
- $30 Standard: 7% (declined because Sweet Spot exists)
- Conversion to paid: 37% (+7% absolute)

Revenue gain: +7% paid conversion = +$5K/month at scale
```

**The insight:** Adding the $15 tier makes $6 look "too cheap" and $30 look "too expensive," pushing people to the middle.
