## With 2.0x Captain Multiplier

```
Captain becomes worth 20% more of team score (60 pts vs 40 pts)

Captain decision becomes MORE consequential:
    
    "Do I captain the mega-whale for consistency?"
    vs
    "Do I captain the growth rocket for upside?"
    
Decision time spent: 5 sec → 15 sec (3x more thought)

Perceived stakes: Moderate → High

Problem: Still same top 5 people optimal, just higher scores.
```

## How Judges Evaluate Your Game

```
Judges look at:
1. Is scoring balanced? (No one archetype dominates)
2. Does it create interesting decisions? (Captain choice matters)
3. Does it have replayability? (Different winners each week)
4. Is it fair? (No hidden mechanics, transparent scoring)

Foresight Current Score:
├─ Balance:        ✅ YES (4 categories, multiple archetypes exist)
├─ Decisions:      ⚠️  WEAK (Captain is low-stakes)
├─ Replayability:  ❌ NO (same top 5 always optimal)
└─ Fairness:       ⚠️  UNCLEAR (scoring hidden from players)

With Recommendations Applied:
├─ Balance:        ✅ YES (unchanged, already good)
```
