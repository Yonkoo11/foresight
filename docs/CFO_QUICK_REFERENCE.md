**Real talk:** This is a solo founder bet. You're risking 18-24 months of your life for:

- **Upside:** $8M-15M exit or $1M+ ARR business you own 100% of
- **Downside:** Zero revenue for a year, lots of personal sacrifice, potential failure

Only do this if:
1. You have personal runway (savings) to live on
2. You can handle isolation (solo work is lonely)
3. You believe in the idea (not just chasing money)
