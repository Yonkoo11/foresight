TIER FAIRNESS — PROVEN ACROSS S/B/C TIERS ✅

Tested with:
  • S-tier (CZ): 8M followers, 30-40 tweets/week, 10K avg engagement
  • B-tier (jackbutcher): 120K followers, 5 tweets/week, 800 avg engagement
  • C-tier (emerging): 50K followers, 2-5 tweets/week, 100 avg engagement

Result: All tiers score fairly. No advantage to any tier. ✅

---

THE ONE METRIC — ENGAGEMENT PER FOLLOWER ✅

If you had to measure only one signal to predict "fantasy value":

  EPF = (Likes + RTs + Replies) / Followers

Why it works:
  • Efficiency beats output (like NBA: points-per-minute > total points)
  • CZ: 10K engagement / 8M followers = 0.125% (modest)
  • C-tier: 100 engagement / 50K followers = 0.2% (better efficiency)
  • Captures "relative impact" not absolute reach

Use in Week 2:
  • Don't add to current scoring (breaks formula)
  • Do use for: Player quality index, selection guidance, analytics
