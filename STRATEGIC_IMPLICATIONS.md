## RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: X API Restrictions (Existential)

**What:** X could shut down API access again (happened to Kaito)

**Mitigation:**
- Move from API to web scraping/archival data
- Partner with Lens Protocol or other on-chain social layers
- Build own social graph over time
- Focus on on-chain data (wallets) not social data

### Risk 2: Influencers Fighting Back

**What:** Crypto KOLs could sue for defamation over "scammer" scores

**Mitigation:**
- Use objective metrics only (on-chain transactions, crypto payments)
- Never claim fraud, use "risk score" instead
- Clear methodology (transparent scoring algorithm)
- Get legal review before launch
- Frame as "educational" not "accusatory"

### Risk 3: Regulation

**What:** Regulators could crack down on prediction markets or gambling

**Mitigation:**
- Keep fantasy leagues as "contests" not "gambling" (sweepstakes model)
- Prize pools from user entry fees, not house money
- Comply with state gaming laws (state-by-state US rollout)
- Partner with established gaming operators if needed

### Risk 4: Market Cycles

**What:** If crypto market crashes, interest in CT alpha dies

**Mitigation:**
- B2B (verification) works in all cycles
- Expand beyond CT influencers to general crypto KOL verification
- Add traditional influencer verification to hedge
- Build network effects (trust is valuable in all markets)

### Risk 5: Competition from Incumbents

**What:** DraftKings, established analytics firms, X itself could copy

**Mitigation:**
- Moat is domain expertise (crypto fraud patterns) + product
- Move fast (get to market before they react)
- Build community (degens care about product, not brand)
- Exclusive partnerships (some KOL agencies might white-label)
