## SECTION-BY-SECTION GUIDE

### **SECTION 1: Competitive Moat Strategy**
**Read when:** Deciding on launch approach, communicating with Kaito DAO
**Key insight:** Kaito users are homeless. We move fast with 72-hour blitz + hinting at token.
**Timeline:** Days 1-3 of launch

### **SECTION 2: The Blur Playbook**
**Read when:** Designing incentive systems, planning tokenomics
**Key insight:** Blur won with opacity (hidden airdrop). Foresight wins with transparency (public CS = public airdrop formula).
**Action:** Design Credibility Score to be aspirational + shareable

---

### **SECTION 3: Points/Airdrop Strategy**
**Read when:** Deciding whether to hint at token, planning messaging
**Key insight:** Hint at token from Day 1, but don't promise. Increases engagement 30-50%.
**Action:** Decide airdrop formula early (even if you don't launch until Month 9)

---

### **SECTION 4: Influencer Seeding Strategy**
**Read when:** Preparing outreach, deciding on incentives
**Key insight:** Tier-based targeting with different pitches. Macro, mid, micro all matter.
**Action:** Create influencer outreach sequence (see FIRST_48_HOURS.md)

---

### **SECTION 5: The Scoreboard Attack**
**Read when:** Planning Week 1-4 narrative and viral moments
**Key insight:** Leaderboard rankings are weapons. Use them to create controversy, status anxiety, aspirational moments.
**Action:** Plan "Foresight 100" launch announcement (Week 1)

---

### **SECTION 6: Network Effects & Flywheel Design**
**Read when:** Understanding long-term growth, planning scale strategy
**Key insight:** Network effects are psychological (leaderboard anxiety), not technical (more users ≠ better predictions).
**Action:** Understand break points (0→50, 50→500, 500→2K) and mitigation strategies

---

### **SECTION 7: Farcaster Strategy**
**Read when:** Planning product roadmap, thinking about distribution
**Key insight:** Farcaster has 40-60K DAU crypto-native users. Frames v2 allows embedded predictions.
**Action:** Deploy Frame by Week 2-3 (expected 5K daily views by Month 3)

### **SECTION 9: Retention Mechanics**

## DECISION TREES

### "Should We Launch This Week?"

**Yes if:**
- All 3 pages working (Home, Markets, Profile)
- CS calculation verified
- Markets settling correctly
- SIWE login stable
- No critical bugs
- Product ready for 5K concurrent users

**No if:**
- Any major bug not fixed
- Markets not settling accurately
- CS calculation math wrong
- Product can't handle scale

→ Read: FIRST_48_HOURS.md "Hour 0-2: Setup & Final QA"

---

### "Should We Hint at a Token?"

**Yes if:**
- You plan to launch token within 12 months
- You want to drive engagement (30-50% boost expected)
- You can explain airdrop formula transparently

**No if:**
- You're not sure about tokenomics
- You think it's misleading
- You prefer to focus on product only

→ Read: GROWTH_STRATEGY.md "Section 3: Points/Airdrop Strategy"

---

### "Are We Losing Users? What Do We Do?"

**If D1 < 5%:**
- Product not habit-forming
- Debug: Why are users leaving? (interviews)
- Pivot to betting exchange OR influencer collab hub
- See GROWTH_STRATEGY.md "Section 11: What If We're Wrong"

**If D1 = 10% (target):**
- You're winning
- Scale partnerships
- Optimize onboarding
- Plan Premium launch

**If D1 > 15%:**
- You've found something real
- Accelerate everything
- Increase marketing spend
- Plan mainnet (Month 3)

→ Read: GROWTH_STRATEGY.md "Section 6: Network Effects & Break Points"
