## ANSWER 3: What is the core game loop?

### THE DAILY PREDICTION LOOP (Day 1 → Repeat)

```
STEP 1: OPEN APP (6 AM daily)
└─ "3 new markets open today"
└─ Push notification with trending influencers
└─ Goal: Create habit-formation trigger

STEP 2: SCAN TODAY'S MARKETS (2 min)
└─ See cards: "@vitalik_eth tweet >3 times today?" YES 1.6x / NO 2.3x
└─ See cards: "@ethereum engagement exceed 500K?" YES 2.1x / NO 1.7x
└─ See cards: "@airdrop_alert go viral (>100K)?" YES 3.2x / NO 1.3x
└─ One-tap interface: Read, understand odds, decide in <30 seconds per market
└─ Goal: Low friction decision-making

STEP 3: PREDICT (30 seconds, $1-10 per bet)
└─ Tap market → Choose YES or NO → Select amount ($1, $5, $10)
└─ Confirm bet → Market added to "My Positions"
└─ Goal: Immediate dopamine (action taken)

STEP 4: BOOKMARK (throughout day)
└─ Optional: Add to watchlist to see live metrics updating
└─ See "@ethereum engagement" counter ticking up in real-time
└─ FOMO trigger: "Oh no, it's hitting 450K, my NO bet might lose..."
└─ Goal: Keep app open, drive daily checks

STEP 5: SETTLE (11:59 PM UTC)
└─ Market closes automatically
└─ Results posted within 5 min
└─ Your positions show: WIN ($12 → +$2) or LOSE ($5 → $0)
└─ Goal: Immediate feedback loop (gambling psychology)

STEP 6: SEE YOUR CREDIBILITY SCORE UPDATE
└─ "Your Credibility Score: 47 (bronze tier)"
└─ "7-day streak: 5 days 🔥"
└─ "Win rate: 58%"
└─ "Leaderboard rank: #347"
└─ Goal: Progress feeling + status signal

STEP 7: REPEAT TOMORROW
└─ OR MISS A DAY → Streak broken (painful)
└─ OR WIN AGAIN → See yourself climbing leaderboard
└─ Goal: Habit formation through streak mechanics
```

### PROGRESSION CURVE (Day 1 → Day 30)

**Day 1-3 (Activation Phase):**
- Onboard: SIWE → Fund wallet with $5 testnet ETH
- Make 3 test predictions on "safe" markets (top influencers)
- See one settle same day, understand mechanics
- CS = 10 (you made 3 predictions, 2/3 correct)
- **Goal:** Prove the loop works in <10 minutes

**Day 4-7 (Competence Phase):**
- Make 5-7 predictions per day (better odds = higher conviction)
- Win rate starts diverging from 50% (some people are good, some aren't)
- Find your edge: "I'm good at morning chaos predictions"
- CS = 47 (you made 25 predictions, 55% win rate)
- **Goal:** Prove you can be better than average

**Day 8-14 (Habit Phase):**
- Check app first thing every morning (like checking prices on Zerion)
- Develop strategy: small bets on unknowns, big bets on high-confidence picks
- Win rate plateaus at 55-60%
- CS = 73, Leaderboard rank = #1,247 (visible progress!)
- 7-day streak unlocked (new markets, bonuses)
- **Goal:** Prediction becomes daily ritual

**Day 15-30 (Obsession Phase):**
- Predict every single day (no-miss streak = status)
- Win rate: 62% (above 60% = genuinely skilled)
- CS = 102 (Silver tier unlock, new badge)
- Leaderboard rank = #347 (now in top 5% of all players)
- **Monetization moment:** "If I maintain 62% for 6 months, I earn ~$240 (testnet ETH equivalent)"
- **Social moment:** Start posting to X: "I'm 62% accurate on CT predictions. Credibility Score: 247. Can you beat my win rate?"
- **Goal:** Addiction + virality

### WHAT MAKES THIS LOOP STICK:
1. **Daily trigger:** Push notification at 6 AM (always fresh content)
2. **Low-friction decision:** Understand in <30 seconds, act in <10 seconds
3. **Immediate feedback:** Market settles same day (unlike stock trading)
4. **Progress visualization:** CS + leaderboard + streak counter (3 forms of feedback)
5. **Streak mechanics:** Break it and it stings (loss aversion psychology)
6. **Shareability:** Your score is braggable ("I'm #347, beat me")
7. **Skill signal:** Unlike pure gambling, good predictors separate from bad ones quickly

### WHY THIS BEATS ALTERNATIVE LOOPS:
- NOT "Draft a team once, wait 2 weeks for results" (current Foresight = low engagement)
- NOT "Just scroll tweets and engage" (no monetization layer)
- NOT "Buy tokens that maybe go up" (friend.tech = speculation, dead category)
- IS "Prove you're smarter every single day" (sustainable gamification)

### RECOMMENDED SCORING FORMULA (Simplified)

I'm choosing the cleanest version that's both intuitive and scales properly:

```
Credibility Score (CS) = (Win Rate % × 100) + (Total Predictions / 10) + Streak Bonus

Where:
  Win Rate % = (Correct Predictions / Total Predictions) × 100
  Streak Bonus = Current Consecutive Days of Prediction, max +50
  CS maximum: 1,000 (for cosmetic purposes, doesn't matter)

TIERS:
  Bronze: 0-350 CS
  Silver: 350-700 CS
  Gold: 700+ CS
```

### WHY THIS FORMULA WORKS

**1. Intuitive**
- 60% win rate = 60 base points (obvious)
- 100 predictions = 10 points (easy to understand)
- Streak = pure habit bonus (clear incentive structure)

**2. Skill-based but not purely**
- If you're 51% win rate (barely above random), CS grows slowly
- If you're 65%+ win rate, CS grows steadily
- But volume matters too (can't just make 10 "high conviction" bets and call it a day)

**3. Habit-rewarding**
- Streak bonus is the ONLY way to climb fast
- Miss one day = all momentum lost

**Historical precedent of failure:**
- Prediction markets have high churn (Augur, Gnosis, early Polymarket)
- Polymarket only worked after viral market (2020 election)
- Most users are "event-based" not "habitual"

### THE NUCLEAR OPTION: What to do if habit doesn't form

**Timeline:** If by Day 14, you have <5% retention and no signs of improvement:

**PIVOT OPTIONS (in order of speed):**

**Option 1: Shorten settlement time (Fast, 1-week fix)**
- Instead of daily markets, do 4-hour markets
- Prediction → Settle in 4h → Repeat 6x/day
- More feedback loops = higher addiction potential
- Pro: Faster feedback, higher engagement
- Con: Data from X might be harder to refresh (but doable)

**Option 2: Add league mode (Medium, 2-week build)**
- Let friend groups create "leagues" that bet together
- "My CT group put $50 total, we're competing as a team"
- Social pressure is stronger than individual motivation
- Pro: Network effects, shared investment
- Con: More complexity, slower build

**Option 3: Switch to sports betting on Twitch streams (Radical, 3-week pivot)**
- "Predict who wins this Twitch raid, this stream viewer count"
- Real-time feedback, faster settlement
- Pro: Potentially stickier (real-time >> 24-hour)
- Con: Lose CT differentiation

**Option 4: Switch to B2B (Realistic pivot, 2-week)**
- Stop chasing consumer gamers
- Pivot to B2B: "Sell credibility scoring to crypto projects as verification tool"
- Less addictive, but more sustainable revenue
- Pro: Revenue-positive faster, defensible moat
- Con: Not a "gaming" solution anymore

**My recommendation:** If you're at Day 14 with <5% retention, **do Option 1 (shorten settlement time)** first. If that doesn't work, **pivot to B2B (Option 4)**. Don't spend 3 months trying to fix something fundamentally unfixable.
