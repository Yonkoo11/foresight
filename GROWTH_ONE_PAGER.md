## THE CORE LOOP (Nir Eyal)

```
TRIGGER → "Friend beat me #45 vs #47"
  ↓
ACTION → "Check scores" (1 tap, 10 sec)
  ↓
REWARD (VARIABLE) → "My rank changed!" (every 30 sec = 1,440/day)
  ↓
INVESTMENT → "Shared team on X, friends watching"
  ↓
LOOP REPEATS
```

**Why Addictive:** Live scoring every 30 sec (vs weekly fantasy) = 10x faster dopamine hits.

**When:** T+4:15 minutes after signup
**What:** User sees their rank on leaderboard + scores updating in real-time
**Proof:** If aha seen → 60%+ convert to Day-2 retention

**If TTA > 5 min or Aha Conversion < 40%:** Fix auth/draft/leaderboard immediately.

## THE VIRAL MOMENT (How Growth Happens)

**Scenario:** User's unconventional pick (e.g., @niche_protocol) goes viral → moves from #150 to #3
→ Takes screenshot → Posts: "I called it. #3 on Foresight. 📊"
→ 500 followers see → 25-50 click → 8-20 sign up (compete against them)

**Engineering Virality:**
1. **Trending Teams** — Show fastest-rising teams (highlight edge picks)
2. **Live Viral Detection** — "Your pick just got 100K engagement! +12 pts. Share!"
3. **Influencer Seeding** — Top 100 influencers appear on leaderboard early (they share naturally)
4. **Prediction Badge** — "📈 Early Caller (predicted 3 viral tweets)" on Tapestry

## WHAT KILLS GROWTH (ANTI-PATTERNS)

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Auth Friction** | 60-70% bounce at Privy modal | "Email or Social Login (No Wallet)" + fallback page |
| **Slow Live Scoring** | 30-40% churn mid-week | SSE every 30 sec + polling fallback + status badge |
| **Unclear Scoring** | 20-30% retention drop | Hover tooltips: "Activity +3, Engagement +4, Viral +1 (why?)" |
| **No Social FOMO** | 50-60% lower retention | Seed demo accounts + influencer seeding + friend import |
| **No Influencer Incentives** | 10x smaller acquisition | Make leaderboard visible, send notifications, badges |
| **Contest Rhythm Too Slow** | 30-40% mid-week dip | Multiple contests (1-week + daily flash) |

## THE WINNING FORMULA

**Foresight's Unfair Advantages:**
1. **Fastest Feedback Loop** — 30-sec scoring (vs weekly fantasy)
2. **Crypto-Native** — Built for CT, not adapted from sports
3. **Influencer Alignment** — They want leaderboard visibility
4. **On-Chain Trust** — Tapestry = immutable, shareable, verifiable
5. **Free-to-Play Funnel** — Proven freemium model

**The Real Game:** Not "fantasy sports." It's **status + credibility game.**

Users want to prove they understand Crypto Twitter better than everyone else. Leaderboard rank = credibility badge.

**Growth Triggers (Rank by Leverage):**
1. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Influencer Seeding** — 2.0 viral coeff, free
2. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Live Scoring Addiction** — 30-sec refreshes drive obsessive checking
3. ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Leaderboard Proximity** — Beat friends nearby (local rivalry)
4. ⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Viral Moment Detection** — Ride influencer viral waves
5. ⭐⭐⭐ **Team Sharing** — 0.3-0.5 organic coeff per share

**Revenue Model:**
- Free contests (funnel)
- 3 paid tiers: 0.1, 0.5, 2.0 SOL (10% rake)
- Premium: $4.99/mo
- Expected: $50-200K/year at 5K DAU
