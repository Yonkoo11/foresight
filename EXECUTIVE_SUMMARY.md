## THE VIRAL MECHANIC: CREDIBILITY SCORES

**Why this spreads:**

1. **Status:** Founding Member #234 is a permanent badge (you want people to see it)
2. **Ego:** Proof your takes are right (you want to share your score)
3. **Competition:** Leaderboard shows you climbing/falling (you want to beat friends)
4. **Dispute:** Low score = defensible roast material (influencers will argue)

**How it compounds:**

```
User A sees credibility scorecard on Twitter
↓
Clicks link, signs up, creates team
↓
Gets Founding Member #432 badge
↓
Creates shareable "proof card" showing score + badges
↓
Posts to Twitter with referral link
↓
Friends see the card, FOMO on founding members, sign up
↓
Friends tell their friends
↓
Network effects kick in (viral coefficient > 1.0)
```

## THE RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### Risk 1: No Influencer Engagement
**Mitigation:** Focus on content + referral loops. One viral thread reaches 100K+. Influencers see traction and engage.

### Risk 2: Product Breaks During Launch
**Mitigation:** Ensure stability before Week 1. Have engineer on-call. Be transparent if issues occur.

### Risk 3: Viral Coefficient <1.0 (Growth Stalls)
**Mitigation:** Adjust messaging, emphasize scarcity (founding members), DM influencers directly.

### Risk 4: Churn Is High (Users Don't Return)
**Mitigation:** Interview early users on Day 3. Adjust product or messaging. Add daily engagement hook (daily predictions).

### Risk 5: Competitors Notice & Copy
**Mitigation:** You have 6 months. Use it to build moat (data, influencer partnerships, brand). Execute fast.
