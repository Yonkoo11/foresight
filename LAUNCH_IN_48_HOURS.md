## IF THINGS GO WRONG (Pivot Levers)

### If Signups Are Slow (Day 3)

**Check:**
1. Is leaderboard working? (People need to see data)
2. Are influencers engaging? (If not, DM 5 more)
3. Is share card working? (If not, fix immediately)

**Pivot:**
1. Post "Founding members closing" early (create urgency)
2. Make a viral roast thread (pick 1 influencer, make it spicy)
3. Change headline to something more provocative

### If No Influencer Engagement (Day 4)

**Check:**
1. Did they see your DM? (Check their recent tweets—are they active?)
2. Is the product actually good? (Be honest)

**Pivot:**
1. Make a roast thread about their tweets (positive angle)
2. Tag them: "Hey @[name], just analyzed your metrics"
3. DM 10 Tier 2 influencers instead
4. Stop chasing Tier 1 for now

### If Tech Breaks (Anytime)

**Quick response:**
1. Tweet: "We're aware of [issue], fixing now. Sorry."
2. Fix it (get your engineer on it)
3. Update: "Fixed! Back to normal."

**Don't hide problems. Transparency builds trust.**
