## CRITICAL QUESTIONS ANSWERED

### Q: Is the $1.3B fraud number real?
**A:** Yes. Multiple sources cite this across influencer fraud (2025). 49% of influencers engage in fraud, 30% of campaigns underperform. Crypto-specific fraud losses likely higher due to undisclosed shills.

### Q: Why would brands pay for verification?
**A:** Because they already do. Fraud detection market is $540M today (growing 23% CAGR to $4.2B by 2033). Compliance budgets exist for crypto marketing.

### Q: Is prediction markets' success relevant to Foresight?
**A:** Yes. Polymarket proves crypto gamification works ($9B valuation, $2.76B monthly volume). Shows consumer appetite for crypto products with real utility.

### Q: What about Sorare's declining revenue?
**A:** Shows unit economics need improvement, but mechanics work. 4M+ users is proof. Foresight has chance to do it better with verification layer.

### Q: Could Foresight actually build the verification layer?
**A:** Yes. It's a combination of: (1) On-chain data (existing tech), (2) Social data (X API but has risk), (3) Pattern matching (ML problem), (4) Simple scoring algorithm. Buildable in 6-8 weeks as MVP.

### Q: Won't X just kill this like Kaito?
**A:** Possible. That's why mitigation is: Move to web scraping + build own social graph + focus on on-chain data (wallets) which X can't control.

### Q: Could DraftKings or Polymarket copy this?
**A:** Maybe, but Foresight moves first. 6-month window before they recognize opportunity. First-mover advantage matters here.
